How do you start a club?
To start/renew an organization or club, complete the anti-hazing form (PDF) and attach it via email, or turn it into suite 428 (EH 4N). Whomever signs the form should be the primary contact of the club (i.e. the president). That's it! You'll be registered as a club.
How can I make purchases?
Want to make a purchase on behalf of a club or organization? Please submit a line item to the line item form ahead of time.
